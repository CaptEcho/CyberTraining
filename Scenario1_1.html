Scenario 1 start


You are a cyber security professional that is currently watching over the systems of a nuclear powerplant's industrial control systems or ICS. This ICS watches over the nuclear powerplant's cooling system. The cooling system is necessary as it ensures the reactor core maintains temperature and doesn't overheat. If it overheats, the reactor core could melt down, causing catastrophic damage to not only the power plant itself, but also the area around the facility as well.
As you continue to work, you slowly realize that the main temperature sensors are indicating that heat is rising heavily within the reactor. However, the back up sensors are indicating temperature levels are normal. What should you do in this situation?
