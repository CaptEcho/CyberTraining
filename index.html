CyberTraining Game for Nuclear Power Plant Workers

Background

Cybersecurity is necessary for a nuclear power plant to continue to function. Hackers regularly assault all sorts of infrastructure, organizations, and much more around the world. In fact, cyber attacks on nuclear power plants have already occurred. For example, Stuxnet, a malicious computer worm, was used to target Iran's nuclear power plant infrastructure, outright damaging or even destroying existing infrastructure.

As such, it is absolutely necessary cybersecurity workers at the nuclear power plant are fully prepared for any potential scenario. That is the purpose of this game.

Description

As stated earlier, this game seeks to train cyber security professionals in what to do in certain scenarios. This game will be a sort of text adventure game. Within those scenarios, cybersecurity professionals will experience situations where they must properly identify the threat and deal with the cyberattacks in question.
